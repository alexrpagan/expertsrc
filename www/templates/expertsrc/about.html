About Data Tamer

Data Tamer is a data curation system that is being developed at M.I.T. in collaboration with NIBR IT's Data Engineering group, Q.C.R.I., and Brandeis University.

For more information about the system, you can read a research paper: Data Curation at Scale: The Data Tamer System (CIDR 2013)

Questions and concerns about this study can be directed to Alex Pagan or Mark Schrieber.
